Engineering

Process engineering will mainly focus on markets where production technology knowledge is the key, like chemical, petrochemical, oil & gas, pharma, agro, pet food, food & beverage markets and automotive industry.
We bring added value to the clients with our expert technology solutions and process design with use of PDMS and other advanced software. Our team of experienced specialists offer technology know-how to optimize client’s process system for better performance, lower cost and maximum reliability. Our main strength towards the clients is understanding their needs.

We are able to support clients with:

• Initial project definition

• Site and production logistics

• All stages of design phase including cost analysis

• Permitting and certification documentation support

• Start-up services for the process plants
